Clan System

Home » Guides » Clan-system
Criteria for creating a clan
  • You must be level 6 (Yellow sergeant) and above.
  • You must have at least 25,000 BP to create a 6-member clan, or 45,000 BP to create a 12-member clan. (There will be option to increase clan member slots in future.)
Creating a Clan
  • Step. 1
    You need to fulfil the criteria above before you could create a clan.
  • Step. 2
    Click on the Clan button at the top menu.
  • Step. 3
    Click on "Create Clan" tab at the Clan screen.
  • Step. 4
    Click on "Create Clan" button to proceed with clan creation.
  • Step. 5
    Fill in your Clan name, Clan initial for the web address (website not available yet), choose your Area (Singapore / Malaysia / Philippines / Others) and your message to the clan.
  • Step. 6
    Choose the default clan size you wish to create. 6-member clan (require 25,000 BP) or 12-member clan (require 45,000 BP). (There will be option to increase clan member slots in future.)
  • Step. 7
    Click on "Create Clan" button to create clan.
Inviting Clan members
  • Step. 1
    Now you have created your clan. You need to invite friends to join your clan.
  • Step. 2
    At the lobby / friend / chat room list, simply right-click on the player's name and select "Invite to Join" to invite your friend to join your clan.
  • Step. 3
    After your friend had accepted your invitation, you need to approve their request.
  • Step. 4
    To approve, click on "Clan" button at the top menu. Click on "Clan Management" tab at the Clan screen. Click on "Yes" button in the request list to approve or "No" button to reject.
Criteria to join a clan
  • To join a clan, you cannot be an existing member of another clan.
  • You cannot have a join request in progress for another clan.
  • Your join request needs to be approved by an authorised clan member in the clan.
Joining a clan
  • Step. 1
    Click on "Clan" button at the top menu.
  • Step. 2
    Click on "Join Clan" tab on the Clan screen.
  • Step. 3
    Click on "Clan Search" button to find the Clan name, enter the full clan name and click on "Find" button to find the clan.
  • Step. 4
    After the clan is found, click on "Join Clan" button to request to join clan.
  • Step. 5
    Once your request is been approved by an authorised clan member of the clan, you will successfully join the clan. At the next login, you will see your clan mark and clan name beside your nickname when you join a game room.
Penalty
  • As a clan member, it will cost you 6,000 BP if you should leave the clan.
  • As a clan master, it will cost you 6,000 BP if you dismiss a clan member.
  • No cost will incurred to clan master if a member choose to leave the clan.
  • No cost will incurred to the member if the clan master choose to dismiss you from clan.
