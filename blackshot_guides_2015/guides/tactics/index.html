Tactics

Home » Guides » Tactics
How to use:

Character is given 5 tactic slots, locked as default. Character will be given some tactic skills and unlocks a tactic slots when you reached level 4, 16, 29 and 39. These slots can be found under Storage > Gear, click on the slot and assign a desired skill you would like to adopt. Each skill can only be assigned to 1 slot.

Tactic Skills:
  • Skirmisher I
    Increases your movement speed when a melee weapon is equipped.
  • Scout
    Increases your movement speed when a pistol is equipped.
  • Berserker
    Increases your movement speed when a shotgun is equipped.
  • Specialist
    Increases your mastery by 15%.
  • Sweeper
    Increases your movement speed when a sub-machine gun is equipped.
  • Guardian I
    Increases your HP by 5.
  • Assasin
    Increases your movement speed when a sniper rifle is equipped.
  • Dominater
    Increases your movement speed when an assault rifle is equipped.
  • Gun Slinger I
    Has a chance of increasing the reload speed of your pistol by 1.5 seconds.
  • Gun Slinger II
    Increases the fire rate of your pistol.
  • Punisher
    Increases your damage when a melee weapon is equipped.
  • Hitman
    Increases your attack speed when a melee weapon is equipped.
  • Guardian II
    [Speed -3%] Increases your HP by 10.
  • Demolition Man I
    Increases your grenades blast radius.
  • Short Fuse
    Decreases the delay for your flashbang to trigger.
  • Blitzkrieg
    Increases the fire rate of your shotgun.
  • Commando
    Has a chance of increasing the reload speed of your rifle or shotgun by 1.5 seconds.
  • Ranger
    Has a chance of increasing the reload speed of your sniper rifle by 1.5 seconds.
  • Demolition Man II
    Increases your grenade damage.
  • Brutality Evasion II
    A chance to eveade a flash bang resulting in reduced blind effect.
  • Advance Scout (cash)
    Improves marksmanship by reducing the recoil of your handgun.
  • Advance Sweeper (cash)
    Improves marksmanship by reducing the recoil of your SMG.
  • Advance Dominator (cash)
    Improves marksmanship by reducing the recoil of your assault rifle.
  • Brutality Evasion I (cash)
    A chance to evade a grenade blast resulting in reduced grenade damage.
  • Subzero Blood (cash)
    Strengthens your focus resulting in less tremble when being shot by another player.
