Garena Carnival 2015 powered by Logitech G and M1 Fibre Broadband 
?Twice the Size, Twice the Fun!

13 and 14 June | Suntec Singapore Convention & Exhibition Centre

 

Dear Shooters,

 

The event that you have been waiting for all year is now here! Join us at Garena Carnival 2015 powered by Logitech G and M1 Fibre Broadband, this time twice the size and twice the fun, with over 8,000 other like-minded gamers participating in a series of exciting programmes that are lined up especially for you. With double the hall space compared to last year ? this will be our LARGEST carnival yet!

To be held at Suntec Singapore Convention & Exhibition Centre from 13 - 14 June 2015, the highly anticipated Garena Carnival will feature a stage with projection mapping technology and advanced lighting technology?the first-ever for a gaming event in Singapore. You will be fully immersed in an intensive and highly engaging gaming experience as you watch top eSports teams battle for the crown at the League of Legends TLC, EA SportsTM FIFA Online 3?s Fantasy Cup ? Road to Thailand Grand Finals, and Blackshot Champion?s Pride Season 3.

 

To foster a stronger carnival atmosphere this year, we have specially flown in celebrity cosplayers, Aza Miyuko and Hori from South Korea, as guest judges and to meet you at the exclusive meet-and-greet sessions. For those who love to get into the adrenaline-fuelled action, you will have the opportunity to challenge your friends at true-blue carnival games such as Laser Tag and Beat the Keeper. Instagram enthusiasts can also look forward to take home their very own photo souvenirs at our instaprint booths. Finally, we round off the fun-filled weekend with Garena?s Largest LoL cosplay competition ? come and check out the elaborate costumes put together by top cosplayers in Singapore as they compete for a prize pool of SGD 5,500!

Play your favourite Garena games at their respective game booths and stand to bring home in-game prizes as well as exclusive game merchandise. Visit our Core Masters booth to have fun and walk away with great prizes. Each ticket will also give you one chance to win the grand lucky draw or stage game prizes from Title Sponsors Logitech G and M1, as well as from Event Partners ASUS and NVIDIA, worth an estimated SGD 15,000!

Ticket sales are now open and you can purchase them through Sistic at S$10 each (excluding Sistic charges). Each ticket entitles you to an exclusive goodie bag filled with limited edition Garena Carnival 2015 merchandise worth over $35.

Bonus: The first 300 attendees for both days will also receive a special ?Garena Master Card? of in-game codes, containing SGD 80 worth of codes from all our participating 8 PC game titles! Woah!
