[Forum Event] BlackShot Fan Art Submission


Good day Soldiers,

Let's art it up!

We are pleased to announce the BlackShot Fan Art Contest! Submit your artwork now and stand a chance to win attractive prizes!

Steps

  • Create a piece of art-work (size 1280x1024 and 1920x1080) with BlackShot theme.
  • The art-work can only be in 2D digital forms.
  • Only JPEG, PNG or PDF format.
  • File name must be your character name (e.g. RainyNight.jpg)
  • Email the entry to  This e-mail address is being protected from spambots. You need JavaScript enabled to view it with the subject "BlackShot Fan Art Contest" along with the following details:
o Your Name:
o Contact Number:
o Garena UID:
o Character name:
o Artwork Title:
o Description of the artwork (3-5 sentences)
  • Entries with incomplete information and wrong email subject shall be considered invalid.
  • You will receive a reply if the entry has passed the requirements.
  • The submission will end at 27 April 2014.
  • Winners will be notified via email or phone call.

Prize

  • 1st Prize: 3000 Garena Shells
  • 2nd Prize: 2000 Garena Shells
  • 3rd Prize: 1000 Garena Shells
  • Top 10: 320 Garena Shells


How will winners be picked?

  • All entries will first be checked for authenticity and should pass the basic requirements.
  • BlackShot GMs will select the Top 10 entries. Out of these, the Top 3 will be decided by popular vote via Forum.
  • Top 10 entries will be posted on BlackShot Forum for voting. The Top 3 entries with the most votes will win.
  • Judging for the Top 10 will be based on 4 categories: Quality, Content, Impact and Creativity
o Quality : How High quality is the rendering? Did the participant apply a good technique?
o Content : Does the piece clearly reflect "BlackShot"?
o Impact : Was this piece able to get the attention of the judges.
o Creativity : Is the piece unique enough to stand out among the rest?


Rules

  • Remember to keep the PSD/SAI file or the original drawing on paper so that it is easier to verify the original creator in case we encounter an ownership issue.
  • No limit in entries per person. But please keep in mind we look for quality, not quantity.
  • Keep photos decent and suitable for all ages.
  • All submitted entries shall become property of Garena SAM and shall have full ownership rights over submitted entries.

Note: Prize will be given out within seven (7) working days... =)
